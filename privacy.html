Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

What We Collect

When you submit a report, we collect:

  • Item description
  • Location (text)
  • Approximate city (from geolocation, if permitted)

We do **not** collect your name, email, or IP address unless you contact us directly.

How We Use It

Data is used solely to match lost and found items within your local community.

Third Parties

We use OpenStreetMap for reverse geocoding and Tidio for optional chat support. Both are GDPR-compliant.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your report by contacting us via the chat widget.
