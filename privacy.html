Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

What We Collect
When you submit a report, we collect: item description, location (text), and approximate city (from geolocation, if permitted).

We do not collect your name, email, or IP address unless you contact us directly.

How We Use It
Data is used solely to match lost and found items within your local community.

Third Parties
We use OpenStreetMap for reverse geocoding and Crisp for optional chat support. Both are GDPR-compliant.

Your Rights
You may request deletion of your report by contacting us via the chat widget.
