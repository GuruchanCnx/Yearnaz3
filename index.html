Questions?

How is my data protected?

All reports are anonymous by default. We use end-to-end encryption for messages and auto-delete listings after 30 days.

What prevents false claims?

Finders and owners must verify via unique tokens, QR codes, or social login. High-value items require photo proof.

Are there fees or finder rewards?

Free for individuals. Venues pay a small monthly fee. Optional escrow rewards for finders (set by owner).
